History

Bullock Texas State History Museum is a three story tall Museum with different exhibitions, some are permanent while others are there for only a period of time. The museum also includes two movie theaters, the star café and a gift shop.

Their hours are from 10:00 – 17:00 Monday – Sunday

Children aged 3 and under get in free. At the museum they have different activities that you can pay for with different prices there is the museum admission giving you access to all galleries/exhibitions adults 17$, student, military, or Senior(65+) 13$ and for youth(ages 4-17) its 11$
