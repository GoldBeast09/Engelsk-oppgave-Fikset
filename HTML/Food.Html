Franklin Barbecue is a restaurant in Austin, Texas that is open only for lunch time Tuesday to Sunday and is closed on Monday. The owner of the restaurant Aaron Franklin has won many awards and been complimented on his good food, in 2015 he won the Beard Foundation Award for best chef and he was the first chef to be nominated and win the award. So many tourists and locals line up for hours just to eat there.

His menu consists of BBQ meats like Brisket, Pork, Turkey, and Beef. You can either get the meats on a plate with prices by the pound or you can get them on a sandwich. There are also some sides and pies you can get and of course drinks including some alcohol(wine or beer). The sandwiches cost around 16$ and meat by the pound costs around 35$

The opening hours are from 11:00 until they are sold out(usually around 14:00 or 15:00) and they are open only Tuesday to Sunday and closed Monday.
