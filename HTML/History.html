History

In the year 1836 there was a battle in present day La Porte and Deer park, Texas. The Fight has been named the battle of San Jacinto It was the final and decisive fight of the Texas revolution and lead to the republic of Texas to end up as an independent country. The Texan army was led by General by the name Samuel Houston and defeated the Mexican army in a fight that lasted 18 minutes. Afterwards there has been erected a 170 meter tall monument at the San Jacinto Battleground state historic site.

Washington-on-the-Brazos, Texas Also known as just Washington is a county in Texas that is known as the birthplace of Texas because it is where Texas declaration of independence was signed.
